## üìù IMPORTANT NOTES:

### For DALL-E (ChatGPT Plus):
- Copy and paste each prompt directly
- Generate 1-2 variations and choose the best
- Download in high resolution

### For Midjourney:
- Use `--ar` for aspect ratio (16:9, 4:5, 1:1, etc.)
- `--v 6` uses version 6 (more photorealistic)
- `--style raw` gives more realistic results
- Generate 4 variations per prompt

### For Leonardo.ai:
- Use DALL-E prompts (more compatible)
- Select "Leonardo Diffusion XL" model
- Adjust dimensions as needed

### For Ideogram:
- Use Midjourney prompts (similar)
- Good for logos with text
- Generate 4 variations free
